TALENT AQUISITION
REIMAGINED

Let your team’s profile do the talking. Using machine learning to build a model of your company’s talent, it’s easy for us to find your next A-player.

Using publicly available data, Lab Recruit builds deep insights into your current employees’ work history, professional interests, and skillset. These models are compared against the roles you’re looking to fill, and brings you a group of solid developers that would make a great fit for your team.

How it works

1
Who are you looking for?
Tell us about your dream candidate. What kind of background do they have? What sort of work will they do? What kinds of tech do they love?
2
Fingerprinting
We do a deep dive into your company’s roster using proprietary machine learning tools. We use all the data your employees have provided about themselves, their history, and what makes them tick to generate a unique and detailed picture of your company’s team. Using this, we’re able to efficiently identify a lineup of candidates for you, as close to ideal as they come.
3
Work Begins
We then activly enagae the top percentile matches on your behalf.
4
Vetting
Don’t worry -- we do our due diligence. Every match suggested by our algorithm is invited to a one-on-one chat with our team before they are handed off to you, just to make sure everything looks right.
