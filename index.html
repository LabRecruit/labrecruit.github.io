talent aquisition reimagined

Let your team’s profile do the talking. Using machine learning to build a model of your company’s talent, it’s easy for us to find your next A-player.

Using publicly available data, LabRecruit builds deep insights into your current employees’ work history, professional interests, and skillset. These models are compared against the roles you’re looking to fill, and brings you a group of excellent developers that would make a great fit for your team.

How it works

1
Who are you looking for?
Tell us about your dream candidate. What kind of background do they have? What kind of work will they do? What kinds of tech do they love? We know it’s important that your potential candidate has as much exposure as possible to your existing tech stack. This way, you can spend less time onboarding and get straight to work. Starting a dev-ops overhaul? Building out a new experimentation team? Let us know what your plans are for your team’s future, and we’ll build them into our model of your ideal engineer.
2
Team Fingerprint
We do a deep dive into your company’s roster using proprietary machine learning tools. Aggregating the public data your employees have provided about themselves, their GitHub history, StackOverflow Answers, Tweets and Meetups they love to generate a unique mathematical model of the type of developer you’ve had success with in the past.

3
The Perfect Software Engineer
Combining your new team fingerprint and everything you’ve told us about your ideal hire we’ll use predictive analytics to quickly assemble a list of brilliant candidates that would make a great fit with your team and goals. Depending on what you’ve provided us with, they might have just the right mix of previous work history, current interests, and tech exposure to make a lasting impact. Chances are, with the right background, they’ll also have an interest in working for you. Good deal, right?
4
Outreich Begins
We’ll get started right away in making initial contact with the developers we’ve identified using hand-crafted personal emails, full of appealing details about your company. Latest funding info, blog posts, tidbits about your culture, or whatever else you think would be interesting to a prospective hire.

Once they’re interested, we do our due diligence. Every match suggested by our algorithm is invited to a one-on-one chat with our team before they are handed off to you, just to make sure everything looks right.

Why LabRecruit?

Speed
Don’t wait for your engineers to find you. Too many platforms rely on candidates making an application. Using our approach, you’ll uncover entire sections of the job-hunter demographic that were previously unaccessible.
Scale
Whether you’re looking for 2 or 20 engineers, we’ll adapt our workflow to suit your scale, and never compromise on our core values or quality of service. We’re dedicated to allocating the resources and time required to build your vision to reality.
Agility
Changed your mind about your hire? Project requirements shifted? No sweat. Our model is easily adaptable to changing requirements and constraints, and we’ll gladly fine-tune our search throughout the process as you see fit.
