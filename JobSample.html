Senior Backend Engineer - Data Team

At Tinder, we are passionate about building software to bring our growing global user base one swipe closer to a new adventure! As a Senior Backend Engineer on the Data Team, you will work closely with product managers, analysts and scientists in a small but fast growing engineering team. You will apply the state of the art AI technologies in the field of deep learning and computer vision to help our users to find their best matches. A successful candidate must have strong technical skills and be highly motivated to achieve product impacts in a fast paced development environment.
In this Senior Backend Engineer role, you will:
Design, develop, and operate resilient distributed services running on ECS or Kubernetes to serve hundreds of millions of users around the world Participate in cross-team critical system designs and provide valuable insights Collaborate with various functional teams to improve user recommendation systems Influence the roadmap and product development of Tinder App and services Recruit, inspire, mentor and develop team members
